My top 3 movies of all-time

The Pursuit of Happyness

I love the ups and downs in the movie. A struggling salesman who never gives up in trying to find true happiness for himself and his son.

Spirted Away

This is a great anime about a girl wandering a world ruled by witches and spirts, where humans are changed into animals.

Soul

Joe, a middle school band teacher feels unfulfilled because of his ambitions to be a full-time jazz musician. On the day he lands the biggest gig of his life, he nearly dies - but then gets the chance to return to his body if he can figure out the purpose in his life.
